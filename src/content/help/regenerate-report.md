# How do I regenerate a report?

If you have uploaded corrected data or want to refresh a report with the latest figures, you can regenerate it without losing the original. Open the Reports page, click the report you want to refresh, and choose "Regenerate" from the actions menu in the top-right corner.

Regeneration uses the same property list, date range, and presentation settings as the original report, but pulls the latest version of your underlying data. The new report appears as a new entry in your reports list with a version label; the original remains available so you can compare the two side by side or roll back if needed.

## When to regenerate

- After fixing a column-mapping error or re-uploading a corrected CSV.
- After adding a missing expense or removing a duplicate booking.
- After changing a property's classification (for example, switching from short-term to long-term rental).
- When a previous reporting period closes and you want to lock in the final figures.

Regenerating a report does not affect any other reports in your account, and it does not consume an additional report credit on metered plans within the same calendar month.
