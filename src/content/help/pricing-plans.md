# What's included in each pricing plan?

StrataReport AI offers three subscription tiers designed to grow with your portfolio. All plans include unlimited CSV uploads, the full column-mapping wizard, and email support.

## Starter

The Starter plan is ideal for owners managing one to five properties. It includes up to five active properties, five generated reports per month, and a 14-day rolling history of past reports. Reports are produced in PDF and CSV format and can be downloaded or shared by link.

## Growth

The Growth plan supports up to 25 active properties and 30 reports per month, plus a 12-month history of past reports. It adds custom branding on PDFs, scheduled monthly report generation, and CSV templates so your team can prepare data in a consistent format across properties.

## Professional

The Professional plan removes the property limit, includes unlimited report generation, and stores three years of history. It adds role-based access controls, single sign-on (SSO) via Microsoft or Google, an audit log for compliance, and priority chat support during business hours.

Every plan starts with a 14-day free trial; no credit card is required to begin the trial. You can upgrade, downgrade, or cancel from the Billing settings page at any time.
