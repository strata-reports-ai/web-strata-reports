# How do I contact support?

The fastest way to reach our support team is by email at support@stratareport.ai. You can also use the "Contact support" button in the Help menu, which opens a pre-filled email with your tenant ID so we can find your account quickly. We aim to respond to every email within one business day, Monday to Friday, 9am to 6pm Pacific Time.

For urgent issues affecting report generation or billing on a paid plan, mention "URGENT" in the subject line and we will triage your request the same business day. Customers on the Professional plan also have access to priority chat support; the chat widget appears in the bottom-right corner of every page when you are signed in.

## Help us help you

When you write in, please include:

- A short description of what you were trying to do.
- What you expected to happen, and what actually happened.
- The name of the affected report, property, or import if applicable.
- A screenshot of any error message you saw.

If your question is about CSV uploads, attaching the CSV (or a small redacted sample of it) lets us reproduce the issue much faster. We never use customer data for anything other than resolving the specific support request.
