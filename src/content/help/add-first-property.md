# How do I add my first property?

Getting your first property into StrataReport AI takes about two minutes. Sign in to your account and open the Properties page from the left-hand navigation, then click "Add property" in the top-right corner.

You will be asked for a few basic details: the property name, street address, number of bedrooms, and the platform you currently use to manage bookings (Hostaway, Airbnb, or Other). Only the name and address are strictly required, but filling in the other fields up front lets the report engine produce richer insights later.

Once you save the property, it will appear in your portfolio and become available as a target when you upload bookings, expenses, or reservation CSVs. You can edit any of these details later from the property's detail page.

## Tips

- Use a consistent naming convention (for example, "123 Bay St — Unit 4") so properties are easy to find as your portfolio grows.
- If you manage multiple legal entities, set the entity on the property record so the right financials roll up to the right report.
- You can add as many properties as your current pricing plan allows; see the Billing settings page for your limit.
