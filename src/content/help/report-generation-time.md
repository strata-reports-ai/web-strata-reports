# How long does report generation take?

Most reports finish in under two minutes. The exact time depends on the number of properties included, the date range you choose, and how many CSV rows the engine needs to process. A single-property monthly report typically completes in 20–40 seconds, while a portfolio-wide annual report covering 50 properties may take up to three minutes.

The Reports page shows a live progress indicator while a report is being generated. You can navigate away and continue using the app; we will email you when the report is ready and the report will also appear at the top of your reports list.

## What happens during generation

1. The engine pulls all reservations, expenses, and ledger entries for the selected date range and properties.
2. It reconciles platform payouts against bank deposits where available.
3. It calculates occupancy, ADR, RevPAR, and net operating income for each property.
4. It compares results to the previous comparable period and flags any unusual variances.
5. It assembles the final PDF and an editable spreadsheet companion.

If a report has been running for more than ten minutes, something has likely gone wrong; cancel it from the Reports page and try again, or contact support.
